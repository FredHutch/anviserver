Metagenomic binning

View

This public project describes the temporal dynamic of an infant gut metagenomic co-assembly in the early days of life, and overlays the identification of microbial genomes using various manual and automatic binning algorithms.

Metapangenomics

View

This public project describes the concept of “Metagenomics” using Prochlorococcus genomes and TARA Oceans metagenomes as a proof of principle. Briefly, Metapangenomics aims at linking the genomic traits and niche partitioning of microbial populations. It creates bridges between cultivation efforts and environmental surveys.
